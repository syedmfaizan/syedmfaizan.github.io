## Define the security outcome

Be specific about the risk being reduced.

## Define users and owners

Security systems often fail when ownership is unclear.

## Design for adoption

The secure workflow should be easier than the workaround.

## Design for failure

Plan for false positives, emergency access, audit trails, and recovery.
