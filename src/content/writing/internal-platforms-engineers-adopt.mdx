Internal platforms fail when they are technically correct but socially inconvenient.

## Adoption is a product problem

Engineers adopt platforms when they make the desired workflow easier than the alternative. Documentation, migration path, support, and reliability matter as much as architecture.

## Principle

A platform is successful when it becomes the obvious default.
