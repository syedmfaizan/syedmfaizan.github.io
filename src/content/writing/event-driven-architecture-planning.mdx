Event-driven systems can reduce coupling, but they also introduce new failure modes. Planning matters.

## Questions to answer early

- What is the source of truth?
- Are events commands, facts, or notifications?
- How will consumers handle retries?
- What happens when events arrive out of order?
- How will schemas evolve?
