## Problem

Access grants accumulate over time. Even when they were originally justified, unused permissions create unnecessary risk.

## My role

I owned least-privilege enforcement across high-risk systems, including analysis, notification, and revocation workflows.

## Approach

The system analyzed usage data, identified inactive grants, notified affected users or owners, and revoked access through controlled workflows.

## Impact

- Covered 200K+ access grants.
- Analyzed 10M+ logs per day.
- Reduced unused access while minimizing operational disruption.
