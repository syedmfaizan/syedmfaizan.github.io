## Problem

Access management becomes difficult as organizations grow. Permissions spread across tools, ownership becomes unclear, and teams rely on manual or inconsistent workflows.

## My role

I led technical design, wrote specifications, guided implementation, and worked with stakeholders to align the platform with security, reliability, and product needs.

## Approach

The platform was designed around clear ownership, scalable workflows, operational safety, and future extensibility.

## Impact

- Improved visibility into access ownership and lifecycle.
- Created a foundation for scalable access governance.
- Helped reduce security risk through better platform-level controls.
