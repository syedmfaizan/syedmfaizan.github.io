## Problem

Applications outside SSO can become blind spots. They may lack clear ownership, consistent onboarding, centralized inventory, and reliable incident-response paths.

## My role

I architected and helped deliver governance workflows to bring non-SSO applications into a more visible and accountable model.

## Impact

- Onboarded 60+ non-SSO applications.
- Closed visibility gaps around application ownership.
- Improved incident response readiness.
